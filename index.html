>what we actually do

Profit Soft LLC focuses on building algorithmic execution software for digital asset markets. Our systems connect to exchanges through API keys that you create and control. These keys are configured with trading permissions only; withdrawal rights are not used or required in the standard setup.

We do not hold, manage, pool or safeguard client funds. We do not accept deposits. We do not operate investment products, investment funds, portfolios, managed accounts or collective schemes. 1.
You keep your funds on your own exchange accounts under your direct control.
